About me

I am a dedicated civil engineer specialising in structural engineering, holding an MSc in Structural Engineering from the University of Manchester. My passion lies in developing innovative and optimal structural designs that meet functional and operational objectives and ensure constructively viable and efficient solutions. Over the course of my career, I have consistently demonstrated my commitment to precision engineering and problem-solving, delivering results that prioritise safety, sustainability, and client satisfaction.

This portfolio shows my journey in structural engineering, showcasing a selection of projects that illustrate my technical proficiency and my ability to navigate complex challenges in diverse sectors, including residential, commercial, healthcare, educational, and oil and mining projects. From collaborating with renowned engineering firms to leading structural design teams, my experience has shaped me into a versatile and solution-driven engineer.
